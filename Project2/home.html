Science Elsevier'22

This is Science Elsevier 2022

The Science Symposium is an annual event held each spring semester as part of AVV's Week of Research and Creativity. The Science Symposium showcases the scholarship and achievements of students from the School of Science and Technology and those receiving funding from the Center for Environmental Inquiry.

This Expo is also a place for students to find mentors under whom they could undertake their research work. Furthermore, the college also provides them support to undertake their research work.
